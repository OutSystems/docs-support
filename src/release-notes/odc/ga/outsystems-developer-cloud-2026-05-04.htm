OutSystems Developer Cloud 2026-05-04

Released on May 04, 2026

New

  • Previously, when using a Trusted Identity Provider ODC provided a secondary verification flow that allowed users to trigger a verification email from within ODC, even if the status from the Identity Provider remained unverified. Now, users whose email status is sent as unverified will no longer be presented with an option to verify their email through ODC. This ensures that email verification is managed exclusively by the external Identity Provider. (RPOR-27722)
