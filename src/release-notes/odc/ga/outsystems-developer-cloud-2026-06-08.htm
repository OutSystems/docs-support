OutSystems Developer Cloud 2026-06-08

Released on Jun 08, 2026

New

  • We improved the permissions display when creating or viewing Organization Roles. The permissions are now organized clearly by scope and functional areas. The major differences include:
    • Introduction of levels: Streamlining categories like support, subscriptions, and organization management under dedicated, high-level sections.
    • User management split: Separating the previous 'User management' into two sections: User and access management (for platform/organization users) and End-user management, to better convey their scope.
    • Improved relevance: Moving specific permissions to different groups (e.g., View audit trails) for better logical grouping.
    This change does not affect existing access levels or user roles. All permissions continue to behave exactly as before. No action is required from administrators or end users at this time. (RPOR-29372)
