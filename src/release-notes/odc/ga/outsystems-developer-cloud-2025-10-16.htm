OutSystems Developer Cloud 2025-10-16

Released on Oct 16, 2025

New

  • Workflow Editor: Internal Reusability
    • The operation “Go to a previous step” has been renamed to “Go to a flow step
    • It's now possible to create "Go to a flow step" that can point sideways not just to a previous activity.
    • This enhancement improves workflow reusability and reduces the need to duplicate activities.
    (RDODCT-3561)
