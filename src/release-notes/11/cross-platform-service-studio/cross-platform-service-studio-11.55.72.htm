Cross-platform Service Studio 11.55.72

Released on May 18, 2026

New in Cross-platform Service Studio 11.55.72

  • You can now right-click the “Server Actions” folder to use the new 'Create Exposed to Client-Side Actions' command, which automatically generates wrapper actions inside a dedicated “ExposedToClientSide” folder and updates their usage throughout your codebase. This structural separation gives you clear visibility into all actions called by the client side, making it easier to review and enforce proper security measures. Available for Reactive Apps only.

    (R11DT-3955)
