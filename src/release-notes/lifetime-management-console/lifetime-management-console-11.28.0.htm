LifeTime Management Console 11.28.0

Released on Dec 22, 2025

New in LifeTime Management Console 11.28.0

  • Added the 'Expose Entities' configuration settings in LifeTime. This setting is a prerequisite for the future ODC Data Fabric Connector for O11 capability, which will soon be available in ODC. (R11CT-4260) (R11CT-4260)
  • LifeTime can now be installed on FIPS-compliant systems. Check the FIPS-compliance section of the System Requirements page for details. (R11CT-4499)
